# IAGov – Intelligent Governance Platform

## Vision
Transform public governance into an economic optimization problem powered by artificial intelligence. The platform maximizes **Surplus National Net (SNN)** – the sum of consumer surplus, producer surplus, government revenue, and value added from natural resources – while minimizing dead‑weight loss from corruption and environmental costs.

## Objectives
- Provide a data‑driven decision‑making engine for ministries and public enterprises.
- Enable real‑time economic impact simulation of policy proposals.
- Offer a modular micro‑services architecture that can be deployed on‑premise or in the cloud.
- Support transparent, auditable AI agents that assist in budgeting, anti‑corruption monitoring, and resource‑allocation.
